Automotive Solutions Overview

The automotive industry demands the highest levels of safety, reliability, and performance from electronic components. 3peak's automotive-qualified solutions are designed to meet the stringent requirements of automotive applications, offering AEC-Q100 qualified components that ensure years of reliable operation in demanding environments.

Our automotive portfolio includes precision amplifiers for sensor signal conditioning, power management solutions for battery management and electrification systems, and communication interfaces for in-vehicle networking. These components are specifically designed and tested to meet automotive standards including EMI/EMC requirements, vibration resistance, and extended temperature ranges (-40°C to +150°C for underhood applications).

Key Application Areas

  • Electric Vehicle Battery Management Systems (BMS)
  • Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS)
  • Powertrain Control Systems
  • Infotainment and Display Systems
  • Body Control Modules
  • Telematics and Communication Units
