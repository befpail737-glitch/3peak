Consumer Electronics Solutions Overview

The consumer electronics market demands products that combine high performance with extremely low power consumption and small form factors. 3peak's consumer-focused solutions are designed specifically for portable and connected devices, offering ultra-low power operation, high integration, and competitive pricing for high-volume applications.

Our consumer portfolio includes micro-power amplifiers with nanoamp quiescent currents, highly integrated power management solutions with multiple rails in single packages, and specialized audio and interface components optimized for mobile and IoT applications. These solutions help manufacturers extend battery life while maintaining excellent performance and reducing overall system size.

  • Smartphones and Tablets
  • Wearables and Fitness Trackers
  • IoT and Connected Devices
  • Portable Audio Equipment
  • Gaming Accessories
  • Home Automation Devices

Typical Consumer Device Architecture

Portable device architecture with 3peak component integration
